Autumn Odyssey A stylish pair of hi-tops that will create envy from how they make the season of fall yours to dominate. Their only downside is that they are difficult to put on and take off. 7/10
Benevolent Businessman A pair of business shoes that present a worker as serious about their craft, and stylish. These shoes unfortunately have laces that end up detracting from their style however one can easily replace them with laces that give these shoes the respect they deserve. 7/10
Benevolent Boss A brilliant multi-purpose shoe that is guaranteed to garner respect and adoration from all that lay their eyes upon these shoes. These shoes can easily be dressed up or dressed down, with the only downside that from extended usage, these shoes can become quite uncomfortable. 8/10
Blue Bandit Stunning and stylish, a gorgeous pair of shoes that will result in many stares and gasps of awe. These shoes can be used as an everydday shoe as well as for workouts as they are fantastically comfortable and provide incredible comfort. Their only downside is that you may never want to wear another shoe that aren't these from their comfort. 10/10
Midnight Marauder Lovely boots that complete an Autumn inspired look. Unfortunately these shoes lack in comfortability and are severely lacking in a originality lending them the title of being a bit of a "basic" pair of boots. 6/10
