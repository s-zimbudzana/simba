On this page we present this month's detailed and non-biased reviews of dress shoes and sneakers from our own research and submissions from subscribers in order to give viewers an informed perspective before investing time and money into shoes.
New Dress Shoe Releases:
Benevolent Boss A brilliant multi-purpose shoe that is guaranteed to garner respect and adoration from all that lay their eyes upon these shoes. These shoes can easily be dressed up or dressed down, with the only downside that from extended usage, these shoes can become quite uncomfortable. On release they will cost $170 retail. 15/12/18
Benevolent Businessman A pair of business shoes that present a worker as serious about their craft, and stylish. These shoes unfortunately have laces that end up detracting from their style however one can easily replace them with laces that give these shoes the respect they deserve. These will end up costing you $200 16/12/18
Midnight Marauder Lovely boots that complete an Autumn inspired look. Unfortunately, these shoes lack in comfortability and are severely lacking in a originality lending them the title of being a bit of a "basic" pair of boots. The price of these lovely boots is $150 17/12/2018
New Sneaker Releases:
Autumn Odyssey A stylish pair of hi-tops that will create envy from how they make the season of fall yours to dominate. Their only downside is that they are difficult to put on and take off. These wonderful hi-top will cost $140 18/12/18
Blue Bandit Stunning and stylish, a gorgeous pair of shoes that will result in many stares and gasps of awe. These shoes can be used as an everyday shoe as well as for workouts as they are fantastically comfortable. Their only downside is that you may never want to wear another shoe that are not these shoes because of their comfort. These amazing comfortable shoes will cost $300 18/12/18
Radical Rogues Simple and sleek style that will garner respect from onlookers.These shoes not only look good, but can feel great from their incredible heel support. Unfortunately these shoes use an outdated mesh structure that can feel rough on users when used over an extended period of time. The Hectic Hi-top will be priced at a reasonable price of $130 19/12/18
