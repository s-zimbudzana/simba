Dress Shoes:
Bodacious Boots A brilliant multi-purpose shoe, that is guaranteed to garner respect and adoration from all that lay their eyes upon these shoes. These shoes can easily be dressed up or dressed down. Unfortunately the only downside that from extended usage, these shoes can become quite uncomfortable. 08/10
Brilliant Boots A pair of formal shoes that present a worker as serious about their craft and simply stylish. These shoes unfortunately have laces that end up detracting from their style however one can easily replace them with laces that give these shoes the respect they deserve. 07/10
Oxonian Oxfords Lovely oxfords that complete any formal occassion and clothing. Unfortunately, these shoes lack in comfortability and are severely lacking in a originality lending them the title of being a bit of a "basic" pair of boots. 06/10
Sneakers:
Blissful Ballers Stunning and stylish, a gorgeous pair of shoes that will result in many stares and gasps of awe. These shoes can be used as an everyday shoe as well as for workouts and particulary basketball as they are fantastically comfortable. Their only downside is that you may never want to wear another shoe that are not these shoes because of their comfort. 10/10
Casual Canvas A stylish pair of canvas shoesm that will create jealousy from many because of how they make any outfit complete and event yours to dominate. Their only downside is that they do not provude a significant amount of heel support. 07/10
Hectic Hi-Tops Simple and sleek style that will garner respect from onlookers.These shoes not only look good, but can feel great from their incredible heel support. Unfortunately these shoes use an outdated cloth structure that can feel rough on users when used over an extended period of time. 08/10
